# School marketing steps to attract more students 
Reference: 
- [School Marketing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BOUIOa5SI8I)

you want more students in your school, but, so do your competitors. high competition now is a big challenge for every private school in india. parents have so many options for their children that they may not even get to you even when you offer the best of everything. this is where school marketing comes in.

The concept of portraying quality education in your school and that will attract more parents and students is flawed, now it doesn't work anymore.
you need a defined marketing strategy that proactively promotes your quality education a strategy that puts your school on the map a marketing strategy that attracts the parents, engages them and compels them to choose your school for their child, creating one such school marketing strategy can be tricky after all there's so much to it from social media marketing to PPC, SEO, banner ads. where does one start? well, we've got you covered.

These are 5 school marketing steps to get you started on the right foot 

### Step 1: take a look at your website
its the 1st impression of your school, so focus on 
- Overall Design
- User experience
- Value Proposition
- Loading Time
- SEO & UX parameters

### Step 2: start publishing

- Target: parents and teachers.
- Content: localized for your target audience, include local keywords. blog posts will increase your brand's online visibility.

### Step 3: get active on social media platforms, 

- Build  strong social media presence across all major platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, twitter ,start posting more frequently with
different types of content from 

    > text 
    > images to infographics
    > animated videos 

### Step 4: have dedicated landing pages 

these are pages where you collect the leads instead of having just one landing page though have multiple each landing page should target individual segments within your audience these segments can be based on any factor like demography income education level and more.

Remember a 35 year old mother would have different considerations than a 45 year old father having multiple landing pages will help you target different segments in a more personalized way final 

### Step 5 : Run paid ad campaigns

- use platforms like google and Facebook to LinkedIn and Instagram.
- Powerful Targeting Audience
- hire Paid Ad Specialist (if required)
- Use Right Message at the Right time.
- Put up Hoardings, Running Newspaper Ads
