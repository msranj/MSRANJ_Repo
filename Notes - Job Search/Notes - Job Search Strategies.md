___________ # Plan 1 : Job Search ____________ 

If you have a certain job role that interests you, then  

    Apply for 15-30 Companies 

    20 : online applications short listed 
    18 : aptitude tests 
    12 : Interview calls 
    6-8 : assessment days 
    2 : Offers 

___________ # Plan 2 : Resume Modifications ___ 

    Make suitable modifications to resume so that it matches the job role expectation. 

___________ # Plan 3 : Quality reflect in Resume_ 

Your resume should talk about 

    - Compliments 
    - Achievements 
    - Proven track record 
    - Client Saved money 
    - Client Made money 
    - Client saved time 
    - Make work easier 
    - Solve a problem 
    - Build relationship 
    - Attract customers 
 

___________ # Plan 4: Cover letter_____________ 

Write a new cover letter for each company.

    Show that you have done research. (latest Merger & Acquisitions, Trade Deals etc ) 

    Keep in concise (1 Page) 

    Content = An opening statement + (2-3 paragraph) + closing statement 

 
#### Classic example 
---------------------
    Ex : A recent report claims Wells Fargo has at least 700 employees who are involved in innovations. As a result of this bank is ready to offer customized financial products to customers. I will be suitable fit for innovation implementation as I embrace change and new challenges and would like to be part of wells Fargo dynamic and stimulating environment.  

#### Opening power statement
-----------------------------
    Why you want to work for the company? 
    Why you will suit the position? 

    " Excellent academic track record & genuine interest in investment banking makes me the perfect candidate for the position. " 

#### Body of the letter
------------------------
    You should explain your qualifications and prove your skills compliment the skills the employer is looking for. 

    Use job description as main guideline. 

    1st paragraph : skills the company looking for 
    2nd paragraph : prove that you are right person for the job.
